People are walking and driving more now than they were in January, but transit usage is nearly half of what it was. Across the entire United States, requests for driving directions are up 34 percent from mid-January, and walking directions are up 18 percent. But transit routings tell a different story, down 47 percent in that same time period. This is according to the latest round of data from Apple Maps, released as part of its Mobility Trends Reports.
